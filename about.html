About Campusview

We host. We host well. And now we capture the moments worth remembering, too.

Our Story

Started with one apartment. Grew into something we love.

Campusview began with a single serviced apartment in Westlands and one stubborn idea: that a stay should feel like a home, not a hotel. Six years later we host across Nairobi, Karen, Diani and Naivasha — and we've added a photography studio to help our guests, hosts and clients tell their own stories.

  • Locally owned, Kenyan team — no franchise scripts
  • Every property visited and styled by us before listing
  • One number, one team, all of your bookings
  • Now offering professional photography for weddings, properties & events
