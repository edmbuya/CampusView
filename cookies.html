Cookie Policy

What cookies we use, why we use them, and how to control them.

Last updated: April 27, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains how Compassview uses cookies and similar technologies on Compassview.co.ke. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that a website saves on your device when you visit. They help the site remember things about your visit — like your preferences — and make your next visit smoother.

2. The cookies we use

Strictly necessary

These keep the site working — they remember your form inputs, manage your session and protect against abuse. The site won't function properly without them.

Performance & analytics

These help us understand how visitors move through the site (which pages are popular, how long people stay) so we can improve it. The data is aggregated and anonymous.

Functionality

These remember choices you make (like your preferred language or location) so you don't have to set them again on every visit.

Marketing

If you've opted in, these help us show you relevant Compassview content on other websites. We'll always ask before turning these on.

3. Third-party cookies

Some pages may load content from third parties (for example, Google Fonts for typography or WhatsApp for our chat button). Those services may set their own cookies, governed by their respective privacy policies.

4. Managing cookies

You're in control. You can:

  • Adjust your browser settings to block or delete cookies — every modern browser has this in its privacy settings.
  • Use private/incognito mode to browse without persistent cookies.
  • Opt out of analytics on a per-site basis using browser extensions or built-in tools.

Note: blocking strictly necessary cookies may stop parts of the site from working as expected (for example, the booking and contact forms).

5. Do Not Track

Some browsers send a "Do Not Track" signal. Because there's no industry standard yet for how sites should respond, we currently don't react to these signals — but we keep our analytics minimal and anonymous regardless.

6. Updates to this policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time as our site changes or the law evolves. Significant changes will be highlighted on this page.

7. Get in touch

Questions about cookies or anything else privacy-related? Email compassview76@gmail.com and we'll be happy to help.
